Your pass will be on the way to you soon. With your pass, you will be able to purchase $5 rush tickets anytime on the day of the show at any of our Participating Organizations.

Please note the following restrictions:

  1. You must be 13-19 years old to use your Teen Tix pass. You may be asked to provide proof of age (such as a school ID or Drivers' License) to purchase tickets, so be sure to bring one along.
  2. Ticket availability is not guaranteed. We strongly encourage you to call the presenting organization's box office and ask if they expect to have Teen Tix tickets available on the day you want to go. You can also purchase your Teen Tix ticket over the phone on the day of the show at some of our venues.
  3. Teen Tix tickets may be purchased with cash only. Credit cards, debit cards and checks will not be accepted (except over the phone).

Please allow two weeks for your Teen Tix pass to arrive in the mail. If you would like to go see a show before your pass arrives, just print out the Temporary Pass at the bottom of this email and bring it with you to the venue. Your temporary pass expires two weeks from today.
